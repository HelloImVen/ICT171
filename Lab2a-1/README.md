### My Conclusion

In this lab, I compared the total cost of ownership between two printers: the Canon MG2570S and the Epson EcoTank L3250. The comparison was based on a five-year period with 750 pages printed per week, giving a total of 195,000 pages. I included fixed costs such as the printer purchase prices and variable costs such as ink, paper, electricity, and maintenance.

The Canon MG2570S had a lower purchase price, but its ink cartridges became more expensive over time because they needed to be replaced more often. On the other hand, the Epson EcoTank L3250 had a higher purchase price, but its ink bottles had a much lower running cost. Overall, the Epson EcoTank L3250 had the lower five-year total cost of ownership and was the better long-term option.
